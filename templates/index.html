Analyzing Crime Trends and Safety Across U.S. Cities (1975-2015)


Project Description:
As a group, we’ve analyzed crime data across major U.S. cities from 1975 to 2015, aiming to provide a clearer picture of crime trends and their impact on communities. Using data sourced from the FBI’s Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) program, we explore how crime rates have changed over time, identifying cities with the highest and lowest crime rates. Our project highlights cities that have seen the most progress in crime reduction and those that continue to face significant challenges. The goal is to offer insights that can guide decisions for families, policymakers, and others concerned with urban safety.
